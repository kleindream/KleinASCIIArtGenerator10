Klein ASCII Art Generator

A lightweight offline ASCII art generator inspired by the golden age of bulletin board systems (BBS), DOS applications and classic internet forums.

Transform ordinary text into stylized ASCII art quickly and easily, without requiring an internet connection.

Features
Generate ASCII art from custom text
Clean and simple interface
Copy generated ASCII art to the clipboard
Clear output instantly
Fully offline
Lightweight and portable
Classic retro-inspired design
Why This Project Exists

The Klein ASCII Art Generator was created to preserve the charm of classic text-based computer culture while providing a simple and accessible tool for modern users.

ASCII art has been part of computing history for decades, appearing in DOS applications, BBS communities, early websites and forums. This project is a tribute to that era.

Usage
Enter your text.
Click Generate.
Copy the generated ASCII art.
Use it in forums, chats, social networks, projects or personal creations.
